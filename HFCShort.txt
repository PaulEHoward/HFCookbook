                             Biscuits Supreme

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 8     Preparation Time :0:15
Categories    : Breads                          Breakfasts


  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  2               cups  all-purpose flour
  4          teaspoons  baking powder
     1/2      teaspoon  salt
     1/2      teaspoon  cream of tartar
  2        tablespoons  sugar
     1/2           cup  lard
     2/3           cup  skim milk

1.  Turn oven to 450. 
 
2.  Sift dry ingredients into medium sized bowl. 
 
3.  Cut in the lard with a pastry blender till like coarse crumbs. 
 
4.  Make a well; add milk all at once.  Stir quickly with fork just till
dough follows fork around bowl. 
 
5.  Rub flour into cloth.   Flour hands.  Put dough on lightly floured
pastry cloth.   Knead gently 10-12 strokes.  Roll or pat dough 1/2 inch
thick. 
 
6.  Dip biscuit cutter in flour; cut dough straight down.  Cut 8 biscuits.
 Put biscuits on ungreased cookie sheet.  Bake for 10 to 12 minutes or
until lightly browned and not "mushy".  Enjoy!

                          Zucchini with Rosemary

Recipe By     :
Serving Size  : 6     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : Vegetables

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  1         tablespoon  olive oil
  1             medium  onion -- thinly sliced & separated into rings
  1              clove  garlic -- minced
  4             medium  zucchini -- thinly sliced
     1/2      teaspoon  dried rosemary -- crushed
  1             medium  tomato -- peeled and chopped
     1/3           cup  chicken broth
     1/8      teaspoon  salt
     1/4      teaspoon  pepper
  1         tablespoon  grated parmesan cheese

Heat olive oil in a skillet.  Add onion and garlic.  Stir fry 2
minutes.

Add zucchini and rosemary.  Stir fry 8 minutes or until zucchini is
crisp tender.

Add tomato, broth, salt and pepper.  Gently stir fry just until
thoroughly heated.

Spoon into serving dish.  Sprinkle with parmesan cheese.

                  Ziti with Tomato Sauce and Mozzarella

Recipe By     :Karen A. Levin
Serving Size  : 4     Preparation Time :0:15
Categories    : One Dish Meals                  Pasta
                Vegetarian

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  12            ounces  ziti pasta -- (penne, or bowtie),uncooked
  2        tablespoons  olive oil
  1              small  yellow onion -- coarsely chopped
  1           teaspoon  garlic -- minced
  28            ounces  canned crushed tomatoes
  1           teaspoon  sugar
     3/4      teaspoon  salt
     1/4      teaspoon  dried hot red pepper flakes
     1/4           cup  fresh basil leaves -- packed
  8             ounces  mozzarella cheese -- freshly ground
  2        tablespoons  balsamic vinegar
                        Parmesan cheese -- for garnish

1.  Cook pasta according to package directions.

2.  While pasta is cooking, heat oil in a large saucepan over medium heat.

3.  Coarsely chop onion, add onion and garlic to oil, and cook for 2
minutes.

4.  Add tomatoes, sugar, salt, and pepper flakes and bring to a boil. 
Reduce heat and simmer, uncovered, for 5 minutes.

5.  While tomatoes are cooking, cut basil leaves into thin strips.

6.  Cut cheese into 3/4-inch-thick pieces and set aside.

7.  Drain pasta, add to tomato sauce, and toss lightly.

8.  Add vinegar, basil, and cheese and toss again.

9.  Serve with Parmesan cheese.
